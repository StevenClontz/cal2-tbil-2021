Activity 2.3.4.

Suppose a 10 kg bucket of water is constantly losing water as it's pulled up, so at a height of \(h\) meters, the mass of the bucket is \(m(h)=2+8e^{-0.2x}\) kg.

(a)

What is the mass of the bucket at height \(h_i=5\) m?

(b)

Assume that the bucket does not lose water to estimate the amount of work needed to lift this bucket up \(\Delta h=5\) meters.
