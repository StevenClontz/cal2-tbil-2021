A frustum is the portion of a cone that lies between one or two parallel planes cutting it.

The surface area of the “side” of the frustrum is:

\begin{equation*} 2\pi \frac{r+R}{2}\cdot l \end{equation*}

where \(r, R\) are the radii of the bases, and \(l\) is the length of the side.
