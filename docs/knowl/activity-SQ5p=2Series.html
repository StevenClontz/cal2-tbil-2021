Activity 4.5.7.

Consider the series \(\displaystyle \sum_{n=1}^\infty \displaystyle\frac{1}{n^2}\text{.}\)

(a)

How does this series to \(\displaystyle\int_1^\infty \displaystyle\frac{1}{x^2} \, dx\text{?}\)

(b)

Draw a picture relating the sum and the integral (try comparing areas of appropriate regions in the plane).

(c)

Argue that the series converges or diverges.
