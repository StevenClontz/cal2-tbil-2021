Activity 3.1.9.

Suppose that \(\{b_n\}_{n=0}^\infty=\{(-2)^n\}=1,-2,4,-8,\ldots\text{.}\) Let \(B_n=\displaystyle\sum_{i=0}^n b_i\) be the \(n\)th partial sum of \(\{b_n\}\text{.}\)

(a)

Find simple expressions for the following:

  1. \((1-(-2))B_{10}\text{.}\)

  2. \((1-(-2))B_{30}\text{.}\)

  3. \((1-(-2))B_{n}\text{.}\)

(b)

Based on your previous answers, solve for the following:

  1. \(B_{10}\text{.}\)

  2. \(B_{30}\text{.}\)

  3. \(B_{n}\text{.}\)
