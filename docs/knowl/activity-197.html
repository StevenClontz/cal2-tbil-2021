Activity 4.1.2.

Consider the sequence \(1, 2, 4, \ldots\text{.}\)

(a)

Which of the choices below reasonably continues this sequence of numbers?

  1. \(\displaystyle 7, 12, 24, \ldots\)

  2. \(\displaystyle 7, 11, 16, \ldots\)

  3. \(\displaystyle 8, 16, 32, \ldots\)

  4. \(\displaystyle 1, 2, 4, \ldots\)

  5. \(\displaystyle 7, 12, 20, \ldots\)

(b)

Find a formula that allows us to move from one term to the next one.
