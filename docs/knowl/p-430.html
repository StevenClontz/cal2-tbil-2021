  2. \(\displaystyle \displaystyle \sum \pi r_i^2\frac{4}{2^{r_i}}\Delta r\)

  3. \(\displaystyle \displaystyle \sum \pi (r_i+\Delta r)^2\frac{4}{2^{r_i}}\Delta r\)
