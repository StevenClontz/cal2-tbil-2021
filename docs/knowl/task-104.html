Task 3.2.5.b.

For each of the following modified geometric series, use your rewritten form from the previous part to determine if they converge, and if they converge, to what value.

  1. \(\displaystyle -7+\left( -\frac{3}{7}\right)^2+\left( -\frac{3}{7}\right)^3+\cdots\text{.}\)

  2. \(-6+\left(\frac{5}{4}\right)^3+\left(\frac{5}{4}\right)^4+\cdots\text{.}\)

  3. \(\displaystyle 4+\sum_{n=4}^\infty \left(\frac{2}{3}\right)^n\text{.}\)

  4. \(8-1+1-1+1-1+\cdots\text{.}\)
