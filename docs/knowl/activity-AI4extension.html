Activity 2.2.15.

Consider the region bound by the lines:

\begin{align*} x & =0,\\ y & =0,\\ y & =3,\\ y & =-x+7, \end{align*}

(a)

Find the surface area of the solid generated by rotating this region about the \(x\)-axis. (Hint: use two integrals for the side area.)

(b)

Find the surface area of the solid generated by rotating this region about the \(y\)-axis.
