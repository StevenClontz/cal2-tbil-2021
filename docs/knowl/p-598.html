To estimate \(f(2)\text{,}\) we consider \(f_{5}(x)=\displaystyle \sum_{n=0}^5 \frac{1}{n!}x^n=1+x+\frac{1}{2}x^2+\frac{1}{6}x^3+\frac{1}{24}x^4+\frac{1}{120}x^5.\) What is \(f_5(2)\text{?}\)

  1. \(f_5(2)=4\left(1+x+\frac{1}{2}x^2+\frac{1}{6}x^3+\frac{1}{24}x^4+\frac{1}{120}x^5\right)=4+4x+2x^2+\frac{2}{3}x^3+\frac{1}{6}x^4+\frac{1}{30}x^5\text{.}\)

  2. \(f_5(2)=1+2+\frac{1}{2}2+\frac{1}{6}2+\frac{1}{24}2+\frac{1}{120}2=\frac{133}{30}\approx 4.4333\text{.}\)

  3. \(f_5(2)=1+2+\frac{1}{2}2^2+\frac{1}{6}2^3+\frac{1}{24}2^4+\frac{1}{120}2^5=\frac{109}{15}\approx 7.2667\text{.}\)
