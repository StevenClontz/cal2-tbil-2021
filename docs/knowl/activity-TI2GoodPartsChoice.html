Activity 1.2.8.

Consider the integral \(\int x^9\ln(x) \,dx\text{.}\) Suppose we proceed using integration by parts. We choose \(u=\ln(x)\) and \(dv=x^9\,dx\text{.}\)

  1. What is \(du\text{?}\)

  2. What is \(v\text{?}\)

  3. What do you get when plugging these pieces into integration by parts?

  4. Does the new integral \(\int v\,du\) seem easier or harder to compute than the original integral \(\int x^9\ln(x) \,dx\text{?}\)
