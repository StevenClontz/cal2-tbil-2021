Task 3.1.10.c.

What do you obtain by plugging the parametric equations into the expression \(x^2+y^2\text{?}\)

  1. \(\displaystyle x^2+y^2=6\sin(\pi x)\cos(\pi x)\)

  2. \(\displaystyle x^2+y^2=-6\sin(\pi x)\cos(\pi x)\)

  3. \(\displaystyle x^2+y^2=9\)

  4. \(\displaystyle x^2+y^2=0\)
