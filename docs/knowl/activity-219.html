Activity 4.2.15.

(a)

For each of the following, determine determine which of the properties: monotonic, bounded and convergent, the sequence satisfies. If a sequence is convergent, determine to what it converges.

  1. \(\displaystyle \left\{\displaystyle 3n\right\}_{n=0}^\infty.\)

  2. \(\displaystyle \left\{\displaystyle \frac{n^3}{3^n}\right\}_{n=0}^\infty.\)

  3. \(\displaystyle \left\{\displaystyle \frac{n}{n+3}\right\}_{n=1}^\infty.\)

  4. \(\displaystyle \left\{\displaystyle \frac{(-1)^n}{n+3}\right\}_{n=1}^\infty.\)

(b)

Of the above sequences, which of them were monotonic?

(c)

Of the monotonic sequences, which of them were convergent?
